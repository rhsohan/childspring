ChildSpring – About Us Page Design Brief
Objective
Create an emotionally engaging and trustworthy page that introduces the heart, purpose, and people behind ChildSpring. The page should communicate that ChildSpring is more than an organization—it is a movement dedicated to ensuring that every child, especially neurodiverse children, receives love, care, support, and opportunities to thrive.
Visitors should understand:
•	Who ChildSpring is
•	Why ChildSpring was created
•	What ChildSpring believes in
•	Its vision and mission
•	The impact it seeks to create
•	The people and initiatives behind the organization
Desired Feelings: ❤️ Warm & Compassionate • 🌸 Inspiring & Hopeful • 🤝 Trustworthy & Professional • 🌍 Inclusive & Community-Oriented.

•	/core/image/backgrounds/
