ChildSpring – Contact Us Page Design Brief
Objective
Create a warm, supportive, and easy-to-use Contact Us page that helps parents, educators, schools, organizations, and community members connect with ChildSpring.
Since the website is fully static and has no backend, database, or form submission system, the page should focus on providing clear contact information and multiple direct communication methods.
The page should communicate:
•	We are here to listen and support.
•	Questions, collaborations, and partnerships are always welcome.
•	Reaching out to ChildSpring is simple and encouraged.
Desired Feelings: ❤️ Warm & Welcoming • 🌸 Supportive & Comforting • 🤝 Professional & Trustworthy • 🌍 Community-Centered & Approachable.

Image Management Requirement
All illustrations, banners, maps, social media graphics, background images, and icons must be loaded exclusively from:
/core/image/
Suggested structure:
•	/core/image/contact/
•	/core/image/social/
•	/core/image/backgrounds/
•	/core/image/illustrations/
•	/core/image/maps/
No external image URLs or CDN-hosted images should be used.
________________________________________
Static Website Requirement
Because the website is fully static:
•	No contact forms or form submissions.
•	Use direct tel: links for phone numbers.
•	Use mailto: links for emails.
•	Use direct links to social media pages.
•	Maps should be embedded using a public map URL.
