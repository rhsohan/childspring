ChildSpring – Our Programs Page Design Brief
Objective
Create an inspiring and professional Our Programs page that showcases ChildSpring's initiatives and communicates that the organization is a movement dedicated to:
•	Supporting neurodiverse children
•	Empowering parents
•	Building inclusive schools
•	Creating community awareness
•	Developing a child-friendly society
Desired Feelings: Hopeful, informed, inspired, and connected.
________________________________________
Design Personality
🌸 Warm & welcoming
❤️ Compassionate & caring
🌍 Inclusive & community-driven
🎓 Educational & professional
✨ Modern & visually engaging
________________________________________
Color System
•	Primary: Indigo Blue #2014B4 – headings, buttons, program titles.
•	Secondary: Dark Teal #004B6D – navbar, section titles, footer.
•	Accent: Coral Orange #EF5733 – CTAs, highlights, hover effects.
•	Success: Vibrant Green #1E9E34 – growth and achievement sections.
•	Backgrounds: #FAFCFF, #F4F7FB, #FFFFFF.

Programs List
1. Special Education for Children with NDD and Autism
Providing specialized educational support and individualized learning opportunities for neurodiverse children.
2. Special Support and Care for Neurodiverse Children
Providing personalized care that nurtures emotional, social, and developmental growth.
3. Child Development and Positive Parenting
Helping children and parents grow together through expert guidance and positive parenting.
4. Positive Parenting and Parents Education
Empowering parents with knowledge and practical skills for raising healthy and happy children.
5. Pre-Schooling
Creating a nurturing and stimulating environment that prepares children for lifelong learning.
6. Community Building and Movement
Building strong communities that support neurodiverse children and their families.
7. Public Awareness and Social Development
Promoting awareness, inclusion, and child rights through community engagement.
8. NDD and Parenting Products, Tools and Resources
Providing educational tools and parenting resources that support child development.
9. School and Teachers Development
Supporting schools and teachers with inclusive educational practices and professional development.
10. Cultural and Extra-Curricular Development
Encouraging creativity, confidence, and social skills through arts and extracurricular activities.
