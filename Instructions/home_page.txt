ChildSpring – Home Page Design Brief
Objective
Create a modern, warm, child-friendly, and professional homepage that instantly builds trust, hope, and emotional connection. The page should communicate that ChildSpring is dedicated to supporting neurodiverse children and empowering parents through expert care, inclusive education, and positive parenting.
Brand Identity: Child Development Center • Educational Institution • Family Support Organization • Healthcare & Counseling Service • Modern NGO.
________________________________________
Design Style
•	Calm, welcoming, and inspiring.
•	Soft rounded corners, large white spaces, card-based layouts, smooth transitions, and meaningful micro-animations.
•	Combination of professional photography and friendly illustrations.
•	Fully responsive across all devices.
________________________________________
Color System
•	Primary: Indigo Blue #2014B4 – logo, headings, primary buttons.
•	Secondary: Dark Teal #004B6D – navbar, footer, section titles.
•	Accent: Coral Orange #EF5733 – CTAs and hover effects.
•	Success: Vibrant Green #1E9E34 – icons and achievements.
•	Backgrounds: #FAFCFF, #F4F7FB, #FFFFFF.
________________________________________
Typography
•	Headings: Poppins Bold
•	Body Text: Nunito / Open Sans
•	Large readable typography, comfortable spacing, accessibility-focused, and mobile-friendly.

Image Management Requirement
All images used throughout the ChildSpring website—including logos, illustrations, program posters, service images, testimonials, banners, icons, backgrounds, and community photos—must be stored and loaded from a centralized folder named:
/core/image/
Rules
•	Do not use external image URLs or CDN-hosted images.
•	Every page and component should fetch images only from the core/image directory.
•	Organize images into subfolders if necessary, for example:
o	/core/image/logo/
o	/core/image/hero/
o	/core/image/programs/
o	/core/image/services/
o	/core/image/testimonials/
o	/core/image/community/
o	/core/image/backgrounds/
•	Use optimized image formats and responsive images to ensure fast loading and better performance.
This image management system will make the website easier to maintain, update, and deploy.
